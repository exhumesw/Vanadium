Private Browsing

This window is in Private Browsing mode, which disables global history, page caching and storing of AutoFill information.

While in Private Browsing mode, please keep in mind:

  • You cannot hide from everyone with a simple window.
  • A government tracking agency may be watching over your network, we can't protect you from them.
  • Check behind your shoulder right now, is there anyone breathing down your neck?

Windows spawned from this window are also private.

Tabs created in this window are also private, and can only be dragged between other private windows.
